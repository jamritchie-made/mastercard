How Mastercard scales
quality experiences.

Copilot, not autopilot.

We are entering a new era of product creation. AI is changing how we design, build, and deliver digital experiences. The pace is accelerating. Expectations are rising. The opportunity is immense. But while the tools are changing, our standards are not.

We believe every product should be thoughtfully designed, expertly crafted, accessible to all, and built to the highest quality.

AI helps us work faster. It helps us automate, generate, analyze, and scale. It does not replace judgment. It does not replace creativity. It does not replace craft.

The experiences we create still require vision, empathy, expertise, and accountability. That is why MADE combines design systems, accessibility, engineering foundations, and AI-powered workflows into a single ecosystem designed for the future of product development.

A future where teams spend less time recreating the same solutions and more time solving meaningful problems.

A future where quality scales alongside speed.

A future where innovation never comes at the expense of trust, consistency, or inclusion.

We are not choosing between craft and AI.

We are building a future where both make each other stronger.

MADE. Mastercard's control layer for product development.

As organisations scale, complexity compounds. More teams, more tools, more AI-generated output. Without alignment, this leads to inconsistency, rework and risk. MADE fixes that upfront. It standardises experience decisions, aligns design and engineering, and embeds Mastercard standards into every output. So teams move faster.

Built for Mastercard

MADE is not a tool. It's infrastructure. It works across design, engineering, product and AI workflows without forcing teams to change their tools or process. It's a shared dependency. Always on. Always consistent.

Across the lifecycle

MADE supports the full product lifecycle: Design → guided by patterns and standards. Build → powered by components and tokens. Run → governed, validated and consistent. This continuity removes fragmentation and keeps teams moving.

Quality built in

With MADE, quality is not a step. It's the default. Every experience inherits Mastercard standards for accessibility, brand and compliance. No retrofitting. No rework. Just predictable, production-ready outcomes.

Real impact

MADE delivers real, compounding value: faster delivery through reusable standards, reduced rework through upfront alignment, lower risk with built-in compliance, and a consistent brand across every product. At scale, these gains become operating leverage.

What is MADE?

MADE is Mastercard's Adaptive Design Ecosystem — a control layer for product development that unifies design systems, accessibility, engineering foundations and AI-powered workflows so every experience ships consistent, compliant and production-ready.

Is MADE just a design system?

No. While the design system is a core part of MADE, the ecosystem extends beyond components and design guidance. MADE also includes accessibility capabilities, engineering assets, AI-powered tools, workflow integration, governance, and product development standards.

Why should my team use MADE?

Teams using MADE inherit Mastercard standards automatically — accessibility, brand consistency and compliance are embedded from the start, reducing risk and rework while accelerating delivery.

How does MADE support AI-driven product development?

MADE ships machine-readable documentation, agents and skills so AI tools can generate experiences that are consistent, compliant and production-ready by default.

Does AI replace designers and developers?

No. MADE's AI tooling augments teams — handling repetitive work and guardrails so designers and developers can focus on higher-value problem solving.
